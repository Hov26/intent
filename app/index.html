Data Privacy Model

Privacy first: 4 pillars of data protection

  • Locality

    No personal information ever leaves the boundaries of the Telecom data center, including raw network equipment logs and inferred behavior profiles.

  • Minimization

    As a GDPR compliant data processor only a minimal amount of information is being processed and stored.

  • Anonymization

    All data is being anonymized upon receiving.

  • Privacy

    Open APIs allow Telecom Operator to filter any data prior to receiving our servers or control it’s precision.
