About us

Intent.ai is a global company with data as it’s core competence. The company has been established in 2017 and since then has expanded its operations to USA, Taiwan and Russia. We value data privacy, therefore, can proudly say that all our products are carefully built to ensure 100% GDPR compliance.

Our main partners are Telecom operators, as we know their pain-points and how to shape new revenue streams in untapped markets.
